How to use it

  1. Drag the link into your Bookmarks Bar
  2. Go to some site from this list and enjoy reading something
  3. Scroll down…
  4. Click your bookmark’s link created in point 1.
  5. Whalla! No more images or photos, or gifs!
  6. Oops! If you found a bug, please report it now!

Ps: if you want to reset the site, just press “F5” to refresh the site, and the images will be there again for you.


How its works

When you press the button to "hide" the header, you are calling this style (css file), which simply "hides" from your web browser all the images, photos and gifs that the site shows each time we are reading content.
